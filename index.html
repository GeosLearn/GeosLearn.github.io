Learning materials for Geosciences

GeosLearn is a teaching related website where you will find lectures & labs materials for some of the UoS I am involved in.

School

The resources available here are primary intended for undergrads enrolled in a Senior Unit in Geology & Geophysics and/or Marine Science within our School here at The University of Sydney.
